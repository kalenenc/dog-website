ABOUT TINA

Tina's Dog Walking was founded in 2016 after one woman decided she wanted to make a career out of helping people take care of their pooches.

Born and raised in the Pittsburgh area, Tina knew at the begining of this year it was time to make a career out of her love for dogs. In her spair time, Tina enjoys reading magazines, going for walks, spending time with her two daugthers, and taking care of her pets.
